¿Por Qué Amatistas Uruguayas?

Desde el Corazón de Artigas

Artigas, Uruguay produce amatistas con la saturación púrpura más profunda saturation found anywhere in the world — a result of unique geological conditions formed over 130 million years of volcanic activity.

Obtenidas directamente de la región de amatistas premier del mundo where extraordinary geological forces created the perfect environment for exceptional crystal formation with unmatched color intensity and clarity.

Este pequeño país sudamericano of just 3 million people is home to the planet's most coveted amethyst deposits, and we provide direct access to these premium specimens for professional buyers worldwide.
